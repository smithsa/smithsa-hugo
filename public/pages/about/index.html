About.

Hey, I'm Sade Smith.

I am an experienced Software Engineer with a degree in Computer Science from Carleton College who is currently working in the Chicagoland area. I specialize in web technologies and am skilled in both back-end and front-end development. As a polyglot programmer, I've worked with many languages; PHP, Python, Ruby on Rails, Elm, Javascript, and Typescript, to name a few, and enjoy learning and using new languages.

Professionally, I've worked in agency settings as a web developer for over three years with a stint in the non-profit space and most recently transitioned into product engineering. I'm passionate about prototyping, problem-solving, and creating engaging, accessible, and resilient user experiences. My professional interests are primarily in software design, web performance, accessibility, and user experience.

As a versatile and pragmatic executionist, I believe I can solve any problem. With my critical thinking skills and creativity, I am confident in developing creative solutions to a wide range of problems. I am driven by my love of creating and the reality that I can contribute to the general welfare of society through my community.
